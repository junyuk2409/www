Based upon Marvel Comics' most unconventinal anti-hero, DEADPOOL tells the origin story of former
Special Forces operative turned mercenary Wade Wilson, who after being subjected to a rogue experiment
that leaves him with aaccelerated healing powers, adopts the alter ego Deadpool. Armed with his new
abilities and a dark, twisted sense of humor, Deadpool hunts down the man who nearly destroyed his life.
